CopydoWell ("the Add-on") is provided by Metalix ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data the Add-on accesses within Google Workspace and how it is used.

What the Add-on accesses

When you install and use CopydoWell, it requests the following Google OAuth scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets — read and write access to Google Sheets that you open the Add-on in, or that you explicitly point it to (via a spreadsheet URL/ID you enter into the sidebar).

The Add-on also uses:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui — to display its sidebar and menu inside Google Sheets.

How your data is used

  • All spreadsheet data the Add-on reads and writes is processed entirely within Google's Apps Script infrastructure, running under your own Google account's authorization.
  • The Add-on copies, sorts, and filters rows between sheets/spreadsheets only at your explicit request, or according to automation (edit-triggered, form-triggered, or scheduled) that you personally configure and can disable at any time.
  • Automation settings you save (source sheet, destination spreadsheet, sort/filter rules) are stored using Google Apps Script's Document Properties service, scoped to the specific spreadsheet you configured — this data stays inside your own Google Workspace environment.

What we do not do

  • We do not operate any external server that receives, stores, or logs your spreadsheet data.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising or profiling.
  • We do not access spreadsheets you have not explicitly opened the Add-on in or provided a link/ID for.

Data retention

Because the Add-on does not transmit data to any external server, there is no off-Google copy of your data for us to retain. Automation configuration stored via Document Properties persists until you remove the Add-on or clear it yourself, and lives inside your own Google account.

Your choices

You can revoke the Add-on's access at any time via your Google Account permissions page. Uninstalling the Add-on removes its menu, sidebar, and any triggers it created.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the Add-on evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the Add-on's data practices can be sent to support@metalix.in.
